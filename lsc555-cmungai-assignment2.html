The Tower - System Analysis and Usability Test


The Tower database on JSTOR is the digital archive of the Catholic University of America student newspapers from 1922 to 2018. This system analysis and usability test aims to assist undergraduate educators incorporate original source materials into lesson plans and learning activities. For context, JSTOR is a nonprofit organization that partners with libraries, museums, and publishers to preserve and provide access to scholarship. Among the 2651 individual collections from institutions worldwide hosted on JSTOR, The Catholic University of America contributes 22 individual collections, including The Tower.

Heuristic Evaluation

Visibility of System Status

The system responded quickly to search commands, allowing the users to see a response to inputs almost immediately. Downloading documents was the one area where the system showed some weakness. After clicking the “Download” icon, a blank screen appears that provides no indication of what action is in progress. For undergraduate students, the lack of a “loading” or progress indicator may cause them to think that their input did not register.
